Dwight D. Eisenhower

Later life

After leaving the presidency in 1961, he retired to his farm in Gettysburg, Pennsylvania. He later wrote his memoirs and remained a respected elder statesman.

Suffered from multiple health issues, including several heart attacks. Some diesases he suffered: Heart disease, Crohn's disease, Arthritis, Hypertension aka high blood pressure

Died on March 28, 1969, at the age of 78.
